My name is Dinara (Dina) Sagitova. I am a software developer from Baltimore, MD.

Before I became a software developer, I studied Biochemistry at UMBC. I fell in love with programming while working at a bioinformatics lab (Erill Lab) as a project coordinator and a database curator. This newfound passion inspired me to earn a degree in Computer Science (BS).

Since then I also worked as a software developer at a financial firm developing a loan origination platform and at the Research Information Technology Systems (RITS) center at Johns Hopkins University.

This is a personal blog to write about software development, and occasionally, about fun things I do in life, in general.
